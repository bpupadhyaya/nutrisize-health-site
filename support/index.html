Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?
On your device, and only there. Nutrisize Health has no accounts and no cloud copies of your health records. See the privacy policy for details.

How do I back up or export my data?
Open System → Data (Data Management) in the app and export your health data as a JSON file at any time. Because data is device-local, export before switching devices.

How do I delete my data?
Use the Data Management screen inside the app, or simply uninstall the app — local data goes with it.

How do I restore my premium purchase?
Purchases are handled by the App Store / Google Play. On a new device, use the app's restore option (or reinstall while signed in to the same Apple/Google account).

Is Nutrisize Health medical advice?
No. The app provides general educational information only — always consult a qualified professional for medical decisions. See the disclaimer.
