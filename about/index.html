About Nutrisize Health

Nutrition and Exercise Intelligence, by EqualInformation, LLC

Nutrisize Health was built on a simple conviction: most of human health is maintained — and improved — through what we eat and how we move. The knowledge to do both well exists, scattered across scientific literature and expert guidance. Nutrisize Health brings it to your pocket, individualized to you.

What it does

The app combines a nutrition library of 4,995 world foods and 75 tracked nutrients with an exercise library of 5,404 movements, layered over 200+ physiological parameters you can browse and track. Ten KPI dashboards — from Nutrition Quality to Body Composition — turn that depth into a clear, color-coded picture of your health, using familiar traffic-light signals: the healthiest choice is always the greenest one.

Privacy as a founding principle

Health data is the most personal data there is. Nutrisize Health is local-first by design: no accounts, no cloud copies of your records, no advertising, no tracking. Your data stays on your device, you can export it any time, and MDConnect lets you share records with your provider strictly on your terms. Read more in our privacy policy.

Who we are

Nutrisize Health is made by EqualInformation, LLC, a software company creating intelligent applications for a better life. The Nutrisize family also includes Nutrisize and Nutrisize Pro.

Where to get it

Nutrisize Health is available on the App Store (iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Vision Pro, Apple Watch) and on Google Play (Android phones, tablets, and Wear OS watches). The core app is free, with optional premium upgrades.

Nutrisize Health provides general educational information only — it is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. See the full disclaimer.
