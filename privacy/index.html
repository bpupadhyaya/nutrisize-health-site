Privacy Policy

Nutrisize Health · Effective Date: June 26, 2026

1 Who We Are

Nutrisize Health is provided by EqualInformation, LLC.

If you have privacy questions or requests, you may contact us at:
Email: privacy@nutrisize.health or privacy@equalinformation.com
Alternative: nutrisize.universal@gmail.com
Contact page: equalinformation.com

2 Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the Nutrisize Health app (iOS bundle identifier: com.nutrisize.health; Android package name: com.nutrisize.health) and related EqualInformation, LLC pages that link to this policy. Nutrisize Health is available on iOS (App Store), Android (Google Play), and as a web application.

3 Information Nutrisize Health May Access or Store

Nutrisize Health is designed as a local-first app. Depending on how you use the app, Nutrisize Health may store information on your device, including:

  • Health and profile information you choose to enter (such as age, weight, height, resting heart rate, blood pressure, fasting glucose, body temperature, oxygen saturation, activity level, goals, dietary preferences, medical constraints, and country or region)
  • Saved health history, notes, and reference-related records
  • Grocery items, meal plans, and exercise plans you create
  • Photos captured for grocery or equipment scanning
  • Physiological parameter tracking data
  • In-app notification preferences and any optional contact information
  • Local app settings, zoom preferences, and feature-access state
  • Feedback mood, message text, and device/diagnostic information (transmitted only when you choose to send feedback)
  • A disclaimer-acceptance record — including the disclaimer version and text, the time of acceptance, an anonymous per-installation identifier, and device/diagnostic information (transmitted once when you accept the in-app disclaimer)
  • Limited purchase-status information needed to reflect an eligible App Store or Google Play in-app purchase

4 How We Use Information

Nutrisize Health uses information primarily to provide app features on your device, including:

  • Personalized nutrition guidance, meal planning, and exercise recommendations
  • Physiological parameter browsing, tracking, and health monitoring
  • Saved health history and local reference tracking
  • Grocery inventory management and photo-based scanning
  • Key Performance Indicator (KPI) dashboards for health metrics
  • Local app settings, accessibility (zoom), and purchased feature access

5 Local-First Design

EqualInformation, LLC does not operate a general Nutrisize Health cloud sync service for users' health records and profile data. The health and profile information you enter into Nutrisize Health is intended to remain on your device unless you choose to interact with external third-party services from within the app, or unless you choose to send feedback (which transmits only the information described in Section 6).

6 Feedback, Diagnostic, and Disclaimer-Acceptance Data

Nutrisize Health transmits limited technical information to a Google Sheets endpoint operated by EqualInformation, LLC in two situations: (a) when you send feedback, and (b) when you accept the in-app legal disclaimer. In neither case does it include your health records, profile data, name, email, or account credentials.

When you send feedback (only after you tap the "Send Feedback" button):

  • Your selected mood and feedback message text
  • App name, version, platform (iOS/Android/Web), and language settings
  • Device model and manufacturer, and operating system version
  • Hardware details such as chip/SoC, CPU core count and architecture, GPU, and total memory (RAM)
  • Screen and window size, storage usage, app memory, locale/region, and timezone
  • Anonymous app-usage counts (for example, the number of saved health records, meal plans, and exercise plans)

When you accept the disclaimer (transmitted once, automatically, at first launch or after the disclaimer is updated):

  • The disclaimer version, the exact disclaimer text shown to you, and a cryptographic hash of that text
  • The date and time you accepted, whether you scrolled through the text, and how long it was on screen
  • An anonymous, randomly generated per-installation identifier. This is not tied to your name, email, or any account, does not identify you personally, is not used for advertising or cross-app tracking, and resets if you reinstall the app
  • The same device and hardware details listed above

This information is used to improve the app, respond to bug reports, and maintain a record that the legal disclaimer was presented and accepted. All transmission occurs over HTTPS. The endpoint is hosted by Google, which may process technical request information (such as IP address) under Google's own policies.

7 External Websites, Chat Providers, and Reference Sources

Nutrisize Health may allow you to open or view third-party websites, chat services, or reference materials from within the app. If you choose to use those third-party services, your device may connect directly to those services, and those services may receive information such as your IP address, device, browser or WebView information, and any information you choose to enter. Those third-party services operate under their own privacy policies and terms, not this Privacy Policy.

8 App Store and Google Play Billing

Nutrisize Health may use Apple App Store In-App Purchase (on iOS) or Google Play Billing (on Android) for eligible in-app purchase functionality. Apple or Google may process payment and transaction information under their own respective policies. Nutrisize Health may receive limited purchase-status or product information needed to unlock purchased content on your device. EqualInformation, LLC does not receive your full payment card number through Nutrisize Health.

9 Nutrition and Exercise Databases

Nutrisize Health includes bundled nutrition and exercise databases stored locally on your device. The app may optionally fetch additional food data from the USDA FoodData Central API or generate exercise variations locally. These database expansion features, when enabled, connect to external data sources but do not transmit any of your personal or health data.

10 Permissions

Nutrisize Health may request the following device permissions:

  • Camera: Used for grocery and equipment photo scanning (only when you choose to use this feature)
  • Internet: Used for feedback submission, database expansion syncs, and in-app purchase verification

Nutrisize Health's core nutrition, exercise, and health features do not require SMS or Call Log permissions. Nutrisize Health is not intended to operate as a default SMS, phone, or assistant application.

11 Sharing and Sale of Data

EqualInformation, LLC does not sell personal or sensitive user data. We do not share users' health data with data brokers or for advertising purposes. Except for user-initiated feedback submissions, the disclaimer-acceptance record described in Section 6, interactions with third-party websites or services, and limited purchase-status handling through App Store or Google Play, the company does not intentionally transmit personal or sensitive Nutrisize Health user data to its own servers or unrelated third parties.

12 Data Retention and Deletion

Data stored by Nutrisize Health generally remains on your device until you delete it inside the app (via Data Management), clear the app's local storage, or uninstall the app. You may export your health data as a JSON file at any time from the Data Management screen. Purchase-related unlock state may also be restorable through App Store or Google Play for eligible purchases. Feedback data and disclaimer-acceptance records submitted to Google Sheets are retained by EqualInformation, LLC for product-improvement and legal record-keeping purposes.

13 Security

Nutrisize Health relies on iOS and Android platform protections, app sandboxing, and on-device storage as part of its local-first design. Feedback submissions are transmitted over HTTPS. However, no method of storage or electronic transmission is completely secure, and the company cannot guarantee absolute security.

14 Your Choices

You may choose what information to enter into Nutrisize Health. You may choose not to use external chat services, browser links, or embedded third-party web content. You may choose not to send feedback. You may remove locally stored app data by using the Data Management screen, clearing app storage, or uninstalling the app. You may export your data before deletion.

15 Children

Nutrisize Health is not intended to knowingly collect personal information online from children through an EqualInformation, LLC-operated backend service. If you believe information has been provided inappropriately, please contact the company.

16 Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and revise the Effective Date above.
